✨ AI Quick Log

With a key set, the bar on the Today screen logs food or workouts from plain language — “3 chicken fillets and a cup of rice”, or “played basketball for 2 hours” (it estimates your calorie burn from your weight & activity). You can also 📷 a photo of your plate. Get a key at console.anthropic.com → API Keys. Your key is stored only on this phone and sent straight to Anthropic — never to this repo or anyone else. (API usage is billed by Anthropic separately from any Claude subscription.)

⌚ Apple Health Sync

Apple won't let a web app read Health directly, so a quick Apple Shortcut sends your workout's calories here. Set it up once (instructions below), and finishing a workout logs the active burn automatically — no typing.
Shortcut recipe (one time):
1. Shortcuts app → + → add action Find Health Samples → type Active Energy, sort by End Date, limit 1.
2. Add Get Numbers from Input (the calories).
3. Add Text = your sync link above, then ?burn=, then the number, then &name=Workout.
4. Add Open URLs (that Text).
5. Run it after a workout — the burn appears here. (Or, in the Automation tab, trigger it “When a workout ends” to make it automatic.)
